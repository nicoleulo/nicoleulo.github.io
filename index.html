How to Use

Getting started takes less than a minute:

1. Enter Your Salary or Hourly Rate

Input your annual salary or hourly wage. You can also set your pay frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly).

2. Select Your Filing Status & State

Choose your tax filing status (single, married, etc.) and the state you work in so the app can apply the correct tax rates.

3. Add Deductions (Optional)

Include pre-tax deductions like 401(k) contributions or health insurance premiums for a more precise estimate.

4. View Your Take Home Pay

The app instantly shows your estimated net pay per paycheck and annually, along with a full tax breakdown.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app free?

Yes, the core calculator is free to use.

Are the tax calculations exact?

The app provides estimates based on standard federal and state tax brackets. Individual situations vary — always consult a tax professional for precise figures.

Does the app store my personal information?

No. All calculations happen on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your income or financial data.

Which states are supported?

The app supports all 50 U.S. states, including states with no income tax.

My calculation seems off — what should I do?

Double-check your filing status, state, and pay frequency. If something still looks wrong, please contact us and we'll look into it.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's what you need to know:

  • No personal data collected: We do not collect your name, salary, or any financial information.
  • On-device processing: All calculations run locally on your iPhone. Nothing is sent to our servers.

  • Advertising: The app may display ads. Ad networks may use device identifiers in accordance with your App Tracking Transparency preferences.
  • No selling of data: We do not sell any data to third parties.
